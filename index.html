About Gram Panchayat

Vadgaon Anand Gram Panchayat is a progressive local governing body located in Junnar Taluka, Pune District. The Gram Panchayat actively works towards rural development, sanitation, transparency, digital governance, tourism promotion and citizen welfare.

The village is known for its cultural heritage, temples, transparency initiatives and participation in smart village development programs.
